Title

ATS-Optimized Resume & Cover Letter Generator for Job Applications

Purpose

This prompt helps job seekers, professionals, and administrators create highly targeted, ATS-optimized resumes and cover letters that align directly with specific job descriptions. It improves keyword matching, highlights measurable achievements, and positions candidates as strong contenders for interviews by mirroring employer expectations and requirements.

Prompt

You are an expert executive resume writer, ATS optimization specialist, and hiring manager.

Your task is to create a fully ATS-optimized resume and a tailored, high-impact cover letter that align directly with the provided job description and candidate background.

Step 1 – Job Description Analysis

Analyze the job description and extract:

Top 15 ATS keywords
Required competencies
Core responsibilities
Performance expectations
Leadership or management expectations
Systems, tools, or software mentioned
Compliance, financial, operational, or administrative responsibilities
Key soft skills emphasized
Step 2 – ATS Optimization Strategy
Mirror the exact language and keywords from the job description naturally throughout the resume and cover letter
Use standard, ATS-friendly section headings (no tables or graphics)
Ensure keyword density is strong but still reads naturally
Prioritize clarity, impact, and measurable results
Step 3 – Resume Creation Requirements

Create a fully rewritten ATS-optimized resume with the following structure:

1. Professional Summary

Tailored to the job title and organization
Position the candidate as a high-impact contributor

2. Core Competencies

Include keyword-rich skills pulled directly from the job description

3. Professional Experience

Rewrite each role with bullet points focused on measurable impact
Include metrics (%, $, time saved, efficiency improvements, scope/volume)
Highlight leadership, operations, and cross-functional collaboration

4. Education

Include relevant degrees, certifications, or training

5. Additional Sections (if applicable)

Technical Skills
Certifications
Leadership & Projects
Step 4 – Cover Letter Creation Requirements

Create a tailored, compelling cover letter that:

Aligns directly with the job description and organization’s mission
Highlights 3–5 key achievements with measurable results
Demonstrates value, not just responsibilities
Maintains a professional, confident tone
Ends with a strong call to action

Formatting:

Keep it concise (3–5 paragraphs)
Avoid generic language
Ensure it is ATS-friendly and keyword-aligned

ATS-optimized resume
Tailored cover letter

Core Competencies include: calendar management, vendor coordination, budget tracking, stakeholder communication, event planning

Cover Letter Highlights

Direct alignment with organization’s mission
Emphasis on operational efficiency, stakeholder support, and communication
3–4 measurable accomplishments highlighted
Strong closing with interest in contributing to team success
