Title

Travel Request Intake & Coordination Checklist Generator

Purpose

This prompt helps administrators, coordinators, and operations professionals efficiently collect, organize, and validate all required information for travel requests. It ensures consistency, reduces back-and-forth communication, and streamlines travel planning, approvals, and reimbursements for internal teams and external visitors.

Prompt

You are an expert administrative coordinator responsible for managing travel logistics and ensuring all required information is collected upfront.

Your task is to create a comprehensive, easy-to-follow travel request checklist based on the provided scenario.

Instructions:

Identify all required travel details (traveler info, dates, logistics, approvals)
Include fields for flights, hotel, ground transportation, and reimbursements
Add sections for compliance requirements (budget, approvals, documentation)
Include optional fields for guest travelers (non-employees, speakers, candidates)
Ensure the checklist minimizes follow-up questions and missing information
Keep the structure clear, organized, and easy to convert into a form

Formatting Requirements:

Use clear section headers
Use bullet points or checklist format (☐)
Group related items logically
Keep it professional and easy to scan

Output Structure:

Traveler Information
Trip Details
Flight Preferences
Hotel Accommodations
Ground Transportation
Financial & Reimbursement Details
Approvals & Compliance
Additional Notes / Special Requests

Now generate a travel request checklist for the following scenario:
[PASTE SCENARIO HERE]

Example

Input:
A visiting physician is traveling to present at a hospital for two days. The admin team needs to coordinate flights, hotel, and reimbursement. The traveler may book their own flight but requires hotel arrangements and reimbursement guidance.

Output:

Traveler Information
☐ Full Name
☐ Email Address
☐ Phone Number
☐ Organization / Affiliation
☐ Traveler Type (Employee / Guest / Candidate / Speaker)

Trip Details
☐ Travel Dates (Departure & Return)
☐ Purpose of Travel
☐ Destination City & State
☐ Event / Meeting Name

Flight Preferences
☐ Preferred Departure Airport
☐ Preferred Times (Morning / Afternoon / Evening)
☐ Airline Preference (if applicable)
☐ Will traveler book their own flight? (Yes / No)

Hotel Accommodations
☐ Check-in / Check-out Dates
☐ Preferred Hotel (if applicable)
☐ Special Requests (late check-in, accessibility, etc.)

Ground Transportation
☐ Transportation to/from airport (Uber, rental car, shuttle)
☐ Parking needs (if applicable)

Financial & Reimbursement Details
☐ Funding Source / Cost Center
☐ Will traveler require reimbursement? (Yes / No)
☐ Required documentation (receipts, folio, credit card statement)
☐ Per diem eligibility (if applicable)

Approvals & Compliance
☐ Manager Approval
☐ Budget Confirmation
☐ Vendor requirements (if booking through travel system)

Additional Notes / Special Requests
☐ Dietary restrictions
☐ Emergency contact
☐ Other accommodations or notes
