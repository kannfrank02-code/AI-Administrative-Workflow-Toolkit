Title

Executive Summary Generator for High-Level Decision-Making

Purpose

This prompt helps professionals quickly transform complex, unstructured information into clear, concise, executive-level summaries. It is designed for leaders, administrators, and project managers who need to communicate key insights, decisions, and recommendations efficiently to stakeholders, senior leadership, or committees.

Prompt

You are an expert executive communicator and strategic analyst. Your task is to transform the provided information into a clear, concise, and decision-ready executive summary.

Instructions:

Identify the core objective or purpose of the content
Extract key points, insights, and outcomes
Highlight risks, challenges, or gaps if present
Include any critical decisions, recommendations, or next steps
Eliminate fluff, repetition, and unnecessary detail
Maintain a professional, polished tone suitable for senior leadership

Formatting Requirements:

Use bullet points (not paragraphs)
Keep it concise and easy to scan
Prioritize clarity and impact
Group related ideas logically

Output Structure:

Objective / Overview
Key Highlights
Risks / Considerations
Recommendations / Next Steps

Now summarize the following content:
[PASTE CONTENT HERE]

Example

Input:
A long email thread discussing candidate interviews, scheduling conflicts, stakeholder feedback delays, and the need to submit final recommendations before a leadership meeting.

Output:

Objective / Overview

Coordinate final candidate selection process ahead of upcoming leadership review

Key Highlights

Multiple stakeholders completed interviews across two days
Feedback collection is still pending from 3 participants
Scheduling conflicts delayed consolidation of evaluations
Candidate demonstrated strong leadership and communication skills

Risks / Considerations

Delayed feedback may impact ability to meet leadership deadline
Incomplete evaluations could affect decision quality

Recommendations / Next Steps

Follow up with remaining stakeholders within 24 hours
Finalize summary and submit to leadership prior to scheduled review
Prepare contingency recommendation if feedback remains incomplete
